Cheesy Green Chile Bean Bake

recipe mexican nytimes chile beans

  • Adapted from New York Times Cheesy Green Chile Bean Bake.
  • Cooking

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb dried pinto beans, rinsed (2×15-ounce cans)
  • 24 oz Flame Roasted Hatch Valley Green Chile (1 jar)
  • 2 (16-ounce) jar salsa verde, or 3½ cups homemade salsa verde.
  • ½ cup packed cilantro leaves, stems reserved and finely chopped
  • 3 cups/12 ounces grated Monterey Jack cheese
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Directions:

  1. Prepare pinto beans per Dried Beans Recipes.
  2. Drain beans and reserve bean broth.
  3. In a stock pot, combine beans, green chiles, salsa verde, and cilantro stems.
  4. Add bean broth, as needed.
  5. Simmer until slightly thickened, 2 to 3 minutes.
  6. Transfer to 9×13 inch pans (2)
  7. Sprinkle the cheese evenly over the top.
  8. Broil until melted and browned in spots, 2 to 3 minutes.
  9. Top with cilantro leaves and a squeeze of lime.
