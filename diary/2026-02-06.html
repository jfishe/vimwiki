Adani Milk Tea (Shay Adani)

tea chai milk

Creamy Yemeni-Style Chai

Derived from Our Yemeni Kitchen, What Makes Adani Tea So Special?.

Ingredients:

6 cups water ¼ cup sugar (or to taste) 2 tablespoons black tea (about 20 grams) ½ teaspoon ground cardamom ¼ teaspoon ground cloves 1 can evaporated milk (350 ml)

Instructions:

  1. In a pot or kettle, add 6 cups of water.
  2. Bring it to a boil over medium heat.
  3. Add sugar and stir to dissolve.
  4. Lower the heat and add black tea, cardamom, and cloves.
  5. Let it simmer gently for 5 minutes.
  6. Pour in the evaporated milk and continue simmering on low heat for 10 minutes.
  7. Skim off any froth from the top.
  8. Strain the tea into a serving teapot or cup.
  9. Serve hot — or pour over ice for an iced chai latte.
  10. Enjoy with family and friends!
