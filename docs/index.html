Santa Maria Rub

tritip rub beef seasoning

Worked well on Tri Tip of beef. Derived from Cookshack topic low sodium steak seasonings or marinades.

  • 1 tablespoon ground mignonette pepper
  • 2 teaspoons granulated garlic
  • 1 teaspoon granulated shallots
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon herbs de provence
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Dutch Baby

breakfast

Adapted from Dutch baby — sweet and savory popover pancakes by Adam Ragusea

  • eggs (one per person, 2 in a 10 in / 25 cm pan)
  • milk (80 ml per egg)
  • flour (20 g per egg)
  • corn starch (20 g per egg)
  • salt (a pinch per egg)
  • sugar (1 teaspoon per egg, sweet or savory)
  • granulated garlic (pinch)
  • vanilla (only for sweet)
  • spreadable cheese, like Rondele Cheese Spread Garlic & Herb, feta, ricotta, brie or mascarpone (OPTIONAL 1 tablespoon per egg)
  • butter
  • toppings (lemon juice and powdered sugar is nice for sweet, eggs/salami/onion is nice for savory)
  • Warm the eggs and the milk before mixing to room temperature.
  • Combine the eggs, salt, sugar, garlic (optional), soft cheese (optional) and vanilla (optional) in a mixing bowl and whisk until frothy.
  • Whisk in the flour until smooth, then whisk in the milk. Or blender all at once.
  • Rest at least 20 minutes before baking.
  • Put your pan cast iron skillet in the oven and preheat to 425ºF/220ºC.
  • Melt in a thick film of butter.
  • Pour in the batter and return the pan to the oven.
  • Bake until puffy and golden (~20 minutes).
  • Add eggs and savory toppings at ~15 minutes, after maximum rise.
  • Return to the pan to the oven until the eggs finish cooking.
  • Transfer to a cooling rack, until steam collapses.
