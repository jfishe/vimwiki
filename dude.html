Blending High Extraction and Low Extraction Flours

The Tartine method calls for wheat flours at various extraction levels which are not easy to purchase. They can, however, be created by blending low and high extraction flour. The Hamelman Challenge, Tech. Note: High Extraction Flour provides a method for blending whole wheat (100% extraction) and typical bread flour (~70% extraction) to achieve the desired High Extraction Flour (85%).
